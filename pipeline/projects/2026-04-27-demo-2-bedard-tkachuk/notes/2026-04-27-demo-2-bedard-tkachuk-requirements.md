# Requirements: Connor Bedard Matthew Tkachuk Panthers Blackhawks hit

## Workflow
1. Script and metadata are generated before clip gathering.
2. Clips are gathered from script cues, then manually approved.
3. Voiceover must complete naturally; never cut mid-sentence.
4. Final export lives in this package's `exports/` folder.

## Visual Rules
- Use real game footage, relevant interviews, and relevant training/workout clips.
- Use official scorecards, standings, rankings, and stat screenshots only when they support the audio.
- Reject gameplay, simulations, fan hosts, podcast panels, subscribe/like overlays, creator title cards, and unrelated faces.
- Avoid visible watermarks by rejecting the clip or cropping/zooming when the shot still works.
- Keep edits minimal: hard cuts, light music under voiceover, no tacky CTA graphics.

## Final Checks
- Full audio completed.
- No b-roll looping.
- No abrupt ending.
- No adjacent same-source clips when avoidable.
