# Banned Topics — Deaner-HD
#
# Inferred from what is consistently absent across 431 videos,
# plus confirmed rules from the kickoff call (Apr 6, 2026).
# The AI checks this file when generating ideas and outlines.

---

## Off-Limit Sports

- No football, basketball, baseball, or any non-hockey sport
- No MMA, boxing, or combat sports (even when hockey players reference them)

---

## Off-Limit Topics / Story Angles

- Politically charged topics — no party politics, social policy, or culture war angles
- Gear reviews or equipment content — no stick reviews, skate reviews, etc.
- Purely speculative trade rumours with no news hook — don't invent scenarios
- Personal player gossip with no hockey relevance (exception: off-ice incidents that directly affect a player's reputation, draft stock, or team situation — e.g. the McKenna bar incident is fair game)
- Non-NHL hockey leagues as a primary subject (AHL, ECHL deep dives, junior previews without NHL draft angle)

---

## Off-Limit Formats

- Game recap of a routine, low-stakes game with nothing notable happening — if there's no story, there's no video
- "Top 10" or listicle-style content — he doesn't do rankings lists
- Pure prediction content with no analytical backing — don't just guess, build a case
- Reaction videos to other YouTubers' content — he reacts to player/team news, not other channels
- Blending multiple unrelated stories into one video — one tight angle per video, don't jump topics

---

## Notes on Why

- The format rules above come directly from what's absent across the full video catalog and from the kickoff call (Apr 6, 2026), where Dean said: *"Don't blend all this stuff together. Because like, if you have like a three-minute video, you kind of want to get to the point... people's attention spans are [short]."*
- The topic scope has shifted (Apr 2026) from Canucks-only game recaps to league-wide stories — routine Canucks game recaps after every single game are no longer the model.
